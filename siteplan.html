AI Startup Website – Site Plan

Site Name

Name: Synapse AI

Reason: The name "Synapse AI" suggests intelligent neural connections, aligning with the concept of artificial intelligence and the brain’s processing power. It's short, memorable, and tech-focused—ideal for branding an AI-based startup.

Site Purpose

The site serves as the digital front for Synapse AI, an artificial intelligence startup offering text-generation APIs and consultation services. It features a real-time AI demo, service and pricing information, client testimonials, and a contact form for inquiries or project proposals.

Scenarios

  • How can I try a demo of Synapse AI's text-generation capabilities?
  • What pricing plans are available for startups and developers?
  • How do I get in touch with the Synapse AI team for a custom solution?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #2d2c91

  • Used for: Headings, buttons, links, and accents.

Secondary Color: #f4f7fa

  • Used for: Page background and secondary sections like testimonials or footers.

Typography

  • Montserrat: For main headings and banners (clean, modern tech look).
  • Segoe UI / Verdana: For body text, paragraphs, and form labels (legible and professional).
